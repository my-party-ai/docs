Connecting FastParty to Claude takes about two minutes. You'll do it once, and then FastParty is available in all your Claude conversations.

<Note>
  FastParty in Claude requires a Claude Pro account or higher. The connection is per user — each person connects their own FastParty account.
</Note>

## Steps to connect

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Claude">
    Go to [claude.ai](https://claude.ai) or open the Claude app on your device.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go to Connectors">
    Click the connector or plug icon in the Claude interface, or navigate to **Settings → Connectors**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find FastParty">
    Search for **FastParty** in the Connector Store. You'll see it listed as an official connector.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Connect">
    Click **Connect** on the FastParty listing.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign in to FastParty">
    A FastParty sign-in window will appear. Log in with your existing FastParty email and password. If you don't have an account yet, you can create one at [app.fastparty.ai/signup](https://app.fastparty.ai/signup).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and authorise">
    FastParty will show you a list of permissions it's requesting. Review them and click **Authorise** to complete the connection.
  </Step>

  <Step title="You're connected">
    You'll be returned to Claude. FastParty is now active. To confirm it's working, ask Claude:

    > "What FastParty tools do you have access to?"

    Claude will list the actions it can take on your behalf.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What permissions are requested?

When you connect FastParty to Claude, you're giving Claude the ability to:

- Create and update your events
- Add guests and send invitations
- View RSVP statuses
- Set up agendas, tasks, food preferences, and potlucks
- Create and manage guest circles

FastParty only accesses your data when you ask Claude to do something. It doesn't run in the background or make changes without your instruction.

## Disconnecting

To remove the connection, go to **Claude Settings → Connectors → FastParty → Disconnect**.

Disconnecting removes Claude's access to your FastParty account. Your FastParty data is never affected — everything stays intact in FastParty.
