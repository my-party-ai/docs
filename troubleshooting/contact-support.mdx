## Before you contact us

To help us resolve your issue as quickly as possible, please include the following when you reach out:

- **Your account email address**
- **Device and platform** — for example, iPhone iOS 17, Android, or Chrome on Windows
- **Steps to reproduce the issue** — what you did and what happened
- **Any error messages** — a screenshot or the exact text of any error you saw

The more detail you provide, the faster we can help.
