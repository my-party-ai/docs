FastParty makes it easy to plan any event — birthday parties, potlucks, work gatherings, dinner parties, and more — through natural conversation with AI. No complex forms, no confusing menus. Just tell FastParty what you need.

## Plan events the easy way

Whether you're organising an intimate dinner or a large celebration, FastParty handles the details: guests, invitations, agendas, food preferences, potluck sign-ups, and tasks — all in one place.

You can manage everything manually through the app, or let **Antsy**, FastParty's built-in AI concierge, do it for you through conversation.

## Meet Antsy, your AI concierge

Antsy is built into the FastParty app on iOS, Android, and web. Describe what you need in plain language and Antsy takes care of the setup — no navigating menus required.

> "Plan a housewarming party for next Saturday at 6 PM, around 40 guests. Set up a potluck with mains, sides, and desserts."

Antsy handles it all in seconds.

## FastParty also works inside Claude

If you use Claude AI, you can manage your entire FastParty account directly from Claude — without opening the FastParty app. FastParty is available as an official connector in the Claude Connector Store, powered by FastParty's MCP (Model Context Protocol) server.

<Note>
  The Claude integration and Antsy are two separate ways to use AI with FastParty. Antsy lives inside the FastParty app. The Claude integration lets you manage FastParty from within Claude itself.
</Note>
