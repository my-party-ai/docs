FastParty offers a free plan and paid plans with additional features and higher limits.

## Plans

### Free plan

The free plan includes:

- Create and manage basic events
- Limited number of guests per event
- Access to agenda, tasks, and food preferences
- Antsy AI concierge (limited usage)

The free plan is a great way to get started with FastParty for smaller events.

### Paid plans

Paid plans remove limits on guests, unlock advanced features, and include full Antsy usage. For the latest pricing and feature comparison, visit [fastparty.ai/pricing](https://fastparty.ai/pricing).

## Managing your subscription

### Upgrading

Go to **Account → Billing → Upgrade** to choose a paid plan. You'll be prompted to enter payment details. Your upgraded plan activates immediately.

### Downgrading

Go to **Account → Billing → Change plan** to move to a lower plan or back to free. The change takes effect at the end of your current billing period.

### Cancelling

Go to **Account → Billing → Cancel subscription**. Your plan continues until the end of the current billing period, then reverts to free. You don't lose any event data.

## Invoices

Past invoices are available under **Account → Billing → Invoice history**. Each invoice can be downloaded as a PDF.
