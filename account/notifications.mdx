title: "Notifications"
description: "Control how FastParty communicates with you."
---

FastParty sends email notifications to keep you informed about your events. You can turn each type of notification on or off from your account settings.

## Managing notifications

Go to **Account → Notifications** to see and adjust your preferences.

## Notification types

### Event updates

Receive an email when a co-organiser makes changes to an event you're part of — such as updating the date, time, or location.

### RSVP responses

Get notified when a guest responds to an invitation. You can choose to receive one email per response or a daily summary.

### Task reminders

Receive reminders for tasks that are approaching their due date or are overdue. You can set how many days in advance you'd like the reminder.

### Potluck sign-ups

Get notified when a guest claims a potluck slot. Useful for keeping an eye on which categories fill up first.

## Turning notifications off

To turn off a notification type, toggle it off in **Account → Notifications** and save. FastParty won't send that type of email until you turn it back on.

<Note>
  Certain transactional emails — such as password reset and email verification — cannot be turned off. These are essential for account security.
</Note>
