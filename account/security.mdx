title: "Security"
description: "Keep your FastParty account secure."
---

FastParty gives you tools to manage your account security and control what third-party apps can access your data.

## Changing your password

Go to **Account → Security → Change password**. Enter your current password, then your new password twice to confirm. Click **Save**.

If you've forgotten your password, use the **Forgot password** link on the login page. FastParty will send a reset link to your email address.

## Logging out of all devices

If you think someone else may have access to your account, or if you've logged in on a shared device, you can end all active sessions at once.

Go to **Account → Security → Sign out all devices**. This logs you out everywhere immediately. You'll need to sign in again on your devices.

## Connected apps

FastParty shows a list of all third-party apps and services that have been granted access to your account — including the Claude AI integration.

Go to **Account → Security → Connected apps** to see what's connected.

## Revoking app access

To remove access for a connected app, click **Revoke** next to its name in the connected apps list. The app loses access to your FastParty data immediately. Your data in FastParty is not affected.

This is the same as disconnecting FastParty from within the third-party app itself.

<Warning>
  If you suspect someone has gained unauthorised access to your FastParty account, change your password immediately and sign out of all devices. Then contact [support@fastparty.ai](mailto:support@fastparty.ai) so the team can help you review account activity.
</Warning>
