Your profile controls how your name appears to guests and co-organisers, and how FastParty contacts you.

## Updating your profile

Go to **Account → Profile** to update your details.

### Display name

Your display name is shown to guests on invitations and to co-organisers on shared events. You can change it at any time.

### Email address

Your email address is used to log in and to receive notifications from FastParty. To change it:

1. Enter your new email address in the **Email** field.
2. Click **Save changes**.
3. FastParty sends a verification link to your new email address.
4. Click the link in that email to confirm the change.

Until you verify the new address, your original email remains active.

### Profile photo

Upload a profile photo from **Account → Profile → Change photo**. Your photo is visible to co-organisers and within the FastParty app. Guests do not see it.

### Timezone

Set your timezone so that event times display correctly for you. FastParty uses your timezone when showing dates and times throughout the app.

Go to **Account → Profile → Timezone** and select your timezone from the list.
