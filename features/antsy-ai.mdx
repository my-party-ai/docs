Antsy is FastParty's built-in AI concierge. Instead of navigating menus and filling in forms, you describe what you need and Antsy handles the setup — all through natural conversation.

## What is Antsy?

Antsy is available inside the FastParty app on iOS, Android, and web. It understands plain, conversational language and can take actions on your behalf: creating events, inviting guests, building agendas, and more.

You don't need to know how FastParty is organised or where to find each feature. Just describe what you want.

<Note>
  Antsy remembers the context of your conversation within a session — you can refer back to "the event I just created" or "the guests I added earlier" without repeating details.
</Note>

## What Antsy can do

**Create events**
> *"Plan a housewarming party for next Saturday at 6 PM, around 40 guests."*

**Add guests and send invitations**
> *"Add my friend Sarah (sarah@email.com) and send her an invite."*

**Build agendas**
> *"Set up a timeline — cocktails at 6, dinner at 7:30, dessert at 9 — and publish it."*

**Configure food preferences**
> *"Set up vegetarian and vegan options and enable them for guests."*

**Organise potlucks**
> *"Create a potluck with mains, sides, and desserts — 3 of each. Then publish it."*

**Create tasks**
> *"Remind me to book the caterer by the 10th and order flowers by the 12th."*

**Answer questions about your event**
> *"How many people have RSVP'd so far?"*
> *"Which potluck slots are still open?"*

## How to use Antsy

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Antsy chat">
    On the mobile app, tap the **Antsy** icon in the bottom tab bar. On web, click the chat icon in the top or side navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe what you need">
    Type or speak your request in plain language. You don't need to use specific commands or formats.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review what Antsy did">
    Antsy confirms what it has done and shows the results inline. You can see exactly what was created or changed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refine through conversation">
    If something needs adjusting, just say so:

    > *"Actually, move dinner to 8 PM."*
    > *"Add one more dessert slot to the potluck."*

    Antsy makes the change without you having to navigate away.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Antsy vs Claude integration

Antsy lives inside the FastParty app. If you use Claude AI and prefer to manage your events from there, see the [Claude Integration](/claude-integration/overview) — it offers the same capabilities but from within Claude, without opening FastParty.
