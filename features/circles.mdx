Circles are saved guest groups. Create a Circle once and you can add all its members to any future event instantly — no need to re-enter names and emails every time.

<Tip>
  Circles are perfect for recurring events — annual family dinners, monthly team lunches, or seasonal gatherings. Set up your Circles once and every future event setup takes seconds.
</Tip>

## What is a Circle?

A Circle is a named list of people. For example:

- **Family** — your immediate and extended family
- **Work Team** — your colleagues
- **Book Club** — your reading group
- **Neighbours** — people in your building or street

Once you've created a Circle, it's available to use across all your events.

## Creating a Circle

Go to **Circles** in your account and click **New Circle**.

1. Give the Circle a name.
2. Add members by entering their names and email addresses.
3. Save the Circle.

You can add or remove members from a Circle at any time. Changes to a Circle don't affect guests already added to past events.

## Using a Circle in an event

When adding guests to an event, click **Add from Circle** and select the Circle you want. All members are added to the event's guest list at once.

If you need to exclude a specific person, just remove them from the event guest list after adding the Circle — the Circle itself isn't changed.

## Managing your Circles

View all your Circles from **Account → Circles**. From there you can:

- Edit the member list
- Rename a Circle
- Delete a Circle (this doesn't affect any events the Circle has already been used for)
