FastParty makes it easy to invite guests, send them everything they need, and track their responses in real time.

## Adding guests

You have four ways to add guests to an event:

- **One at a time** — enter a name and email address manually.
- **Bulk entry** — paste in multiple names and emails at once.
- **CSV import** — upload a spreadsheet with guest details.
- **From a Circle** — add an entire saved guest group in one click. [Learn about Circles](/features/circles).

## Sending invitations

When you add a guest, FastParty sends them an invitation email automatically. The email includes the event name, date, time, location, and a personal RSVP link.

<Note>
  Guests do not need a FastParty account to RSVP. They click the link in the email and respond directly.
</Note>

If you'd prefer to add guests without sending invitations immediately, you can hold invitations and send them manually when you're ready.

## RSVP tracking

Each guest has one of four RSVP statuses:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Pending** | Invitation sent, no response yet |
| **Accepted** | Guest is coming |
| **Declined** | Guest can't make it |
| **Maybe** | Guest is unsure |

Your event dashboard shows a live RSVP count. You can filter the guest list by status and send a reminder to anyone who hasn't responded yet.

## Guest details

For each guest you can see:

- Name and email address
- RSVP status
- Dietary preference (if food preferences are enabled)
- Potluck contribution (if potluck is enabled)

## Removing guests

You can remove a guest from an event at any time. If they've already received an invitation, they'll get an email letting them know they've been removed.

<Tip>
  Use Circles to save groups of guests you invite regularly — family, work team, book club — so you can add everyone in one click for future events.
</Tip>
